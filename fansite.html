History

The Charlotte Hornets are an American professional basketball team based in Charlotte, North Carolina.The Hornets compete in the National Basketball Association (NBA). The team is largely owned by retired NBA player Michael Jordan, who acquired a controlling interest in the team in 2010. The Hornets play their home games at the Spectrum Center in Uptown Charlotte.
